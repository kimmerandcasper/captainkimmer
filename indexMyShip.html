About the Sparkman & Stephens 34 (1970)

The Sparkman & Stephens 34, a masterpiece from 1970, is a boat built for adventure. Known for its stunning beauty and unparalleled performance, the S&S 34 has proven itself on the open water for decades. Its sharp design and solid construction give it the agility of a racing yacht, while its interior comforts provide a cozy retreat for long voyages. Whether cruising in calm seas or braving the more challenging waters, this iconic vessel continues to turn heads and inspire sailors around the world.
